About CERN

CERN is a NFT project that explores the concept of interdimensional reptilians taking over earth. Our NFTs are designed to showcase the different ways in which these reptilians have infiltrated our society.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate developers and artists who believe in the power of NFTs to tell compelling stories. Our goal is to create unique and thought-provoking NFTs that will capture the imagination of our audience.
